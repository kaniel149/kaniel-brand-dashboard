Tuesday — QUICK TIP
AI Tip of the Day
Stop Saying "Write Me..."
Start Saying "You Are My..."
Give AI a ROLE before giving it a task. The output quality jumps 10x.
Instead of: "Write an email to a client"

Try: "You are my executive assistant at a solar energy company. You know my communication style is direct but warm. Write an email to a client who hasn't paid in 30 days."
