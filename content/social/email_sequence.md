## Technical Notes

- Trigger: Form submission on opt-in page (/#/he/guide or /#/en/guide)
- Automation: 5-email drip sequence
- Timing: Immediate, Day 2, Day 4, Day 6, Day 8
- Segment by language (Hebrew vs English) based on opt-in page
- Tag: "guide-download", "email-sequence"

### Tracking:
- Open rates per email
- Click-through rates on CTAs
- Conversion rate on Email 4 and Email 5
- Unsubscribe rate

- Email 1 subject: "המדריך שלך מוכן" vs "המדריך + מתנה שלא ציפית"
- Email 4 subject: with personal story vs without
