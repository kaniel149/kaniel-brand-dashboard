# WhatsApp 5-Day Drip Messages

## Strategy
- Target: People who downloaded the free guide but didn't purchase the course
- Tone: Personal, short, conversational - like texting a friend
- Each message should feel like it's personally from Kaniel
- Include one actionable value piece per message
- Hebrew primary, English translations included

## DAY 2 - One AI Tip

### Message:

**Hebrew:**
היי [שם] - טיפ AI מהיר שחוסך לי 20 דקות כל יום:

כל בוקר אני שולח ל-Claude את 3 המשימות הכי חשובות שלי ליום. הוא מפרק כל אחת לצעדים קטנים ומסדר אותן לפי סדר.

תנסה את זה:

"הנה 3 המשימות שלי להיום:
1. [משימה 1]
2. [משימה 2]
3. [משימה 3]

תפרק כל אחת ל-3 צעדים קטנים. תסדר לפי סדר - מה הכי דחוף קודם. ותגיד לי כמה זמן כל צעד לוקח."

פשוט, אבל משנה את היום. תנסה.

**English:**
Hey [Name] - quick AI tip that saves me 20 minutes every day:

Every morning I send Claude my 3 most important tasks for the day. It breaks each one into small steps and orders them.

Try this:

"Here are my 3 tasks for today:
1. [Task 1]
2. [Task 2]
3. [Task 3]

Break each into 3 small steps. Order by urgency - most urgent first. Tell me how long each step takes."

Simple, but it changes the day. Try it.

## Technical Notes

### Sending Rules:
- Use WhatsApp Business API or manual sending
- Send between 9:00-11:00 AM recipient's local time
- If they respond at any point, switch to personal conversation (exit drip)
- If they say "stop" or equivalent, immediately stop sequence
- Day 1: Voice note + text. Days 2-5: Text only.

### Triggers:
- Entry: Downloaded guide but no purchase after 24 hours
- Exit: Purchased course OR replied "stop" OR replied with conversation (manual takeover)

### Tracking:
- Message read receipts
- Response rate per day
- Conversion rate (purchased after drip)
- Best performing day (which message gets most responses)

### Personal Code:
- WHATSAPP20 = 20% discount
- Valid for 48 hours after Day 5 message
- Track redemption rate
