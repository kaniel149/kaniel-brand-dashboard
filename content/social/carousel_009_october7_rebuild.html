Slide 7 / 9 — REBUILDING
Building Again
AI-First.
From scratch.
Security first. Then systems. Then abundance. Building a business and life where AI handles the noise and I handle what matters: family, creation, growth.
Slide 8 / 9 — LESSONS
What I Learned
LESSON 1
Changing location doesn't fix a broken system
Copenhagen proved that. The problem was internal.
LESSON 2
Security must come before ambition
You can't build when you're surviving. Create buffers first.
LESSON 3
Life is too short for systems that don't serve you
Build for the life you want, not the life you inherited.
