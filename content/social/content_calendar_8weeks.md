# 8-Week Content Calendar

## Strategy Overview
- **Weeks 1-2:** Foundation (introduce yourself, your story, your why)
- **Weeks 3-4:** Value (AI tips, frameworks, how-tos)
- **Weeks 5-6:** Depth (detailed tutorials, case studies, before/after)
- **Weeks 7-8:** Conversion (course promotion, testimonials, urgency)

### Content Pillar Key:
- [AI] = AI First Life (~30%)
- [SYS] = Systems & Order (~25%)
- [S2A] = Survival to Abundance (~20%)
- [LB] = Lifestyle Business (~20%)
- [ME] = Personal / Behind the Scenes (~5%)

### Posting Schedule: Sunday-Thursday (Israeli work week alignment + global)
### Format: R = Reel, C = Carousel, S = Stories (post daily alongside main content)
