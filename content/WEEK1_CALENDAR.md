## ⚡ QUICK ACTION: How to Schedule on getLate.dev

### Per video:
1. Go to https://getlate.dev/dashboard/posts
2. Click "New Post"
3. Upload the video file
4. Paste the caption from above
5. Set date and time (IST times listed above)
6. Select platform: Instagram → Reels
7. Also add: TikTok (for Reels only, not carousel)
8. Hit Schedule

Stories are best posted live (not scheduled) for authenticity.
Use the templates above as a guide - post from your phone during the day.
