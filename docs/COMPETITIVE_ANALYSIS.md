# Competitive Landscape Analysis - AI Agent Courses & Products
## February 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The AI agent education market is **booming but fragmented**. Most courses fall into two categories:
1. **Technical/Developer-focused** (API, coding, frameworks) - oversaturated
2. **Generic "AI for business"** (surface-level, tool demos) - low value

**The massive gap:** Nobody is teaching **"AI-First Life Design"** -- how to use AI agents to rebuild your ENTIRE life system (personal + business + health + family) from a place of intentionality. The intersection of **lifestyle design + AI agents + systems thinking** is essentially unoccupied.
